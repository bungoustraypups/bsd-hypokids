General Appearance: See attached image. Aside from what's in the image, she has a fairly robust and muscular body type, and her typical manner of dress usually includes overalls and/or some item of clothing being made of jean material.

Dislikes: schoolwork, chores, being bored

Parents: Mark Twain (carried her, bio father), John Steinbeck (bio father); Lucy Maud Montgomery (stepmother thru Mark), Louisa "Lou" May Alcott (stepparent thru Mark), Howard Phillips Lovecraft (stepparent thru John)

Who they like better: Mark

Who they take after more: About equal

Personal Head canon: She's a big fan of horses, and keeps at least one at the Lovecraft Steinbeck house.

Early Life: Clara was born following a short-lived relationship between Mark and John shortly before the Guild recruited Lovecraft, which happened shortly before they went to Yokohama; Mark actually found out he was pregnant with her after Atsushi escaped from the Moby Dick (Francis was far more panicked about this than Mark and John combined). By that point, they hadn't been dating for a while, and were really only "dating" before because they mistook their platonic feelings for romantic ones.

After the Guild was defeated, John went with Mark back to his hometown of Florida, Missouri (yes that's the town's name, look it up, it's still there today and has the same name) where Clara was born a few months later. Mark intended to stay there to write his autobiography, and was fine with John moving further north statewise with Lovecraft when they reconnected as he had the support of his family, but eventually realized he missed his former teammates too much to stay, and took Clara with him back to Yokohama, where he reconnected with the rest of the Guild. Shortly thereafter, his relationship with Lucy and Louisa started, and since Clara was only a few months old at that time, she's effectively grown up knowing all three of them as the parents who raised her.

Currently, Clara primarily lives with Mark, Lucy, and Louisa, and while she definitely loves John, she feels more than a little awkward around him and Lovecraft at times, given that she didn't properly grow up with them, and they only moved to Yokohama semi-permanently a few years ago.

Notes: She's dark-skinned because Mark is black latino in my headcanons and is thus also dark-skinned. Also John is half-latino half-white so she, altogether, is black, latino, and white (though less white than black and latino, given only John is half-white).
